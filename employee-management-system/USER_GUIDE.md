# Employee Management System - User Guide

Welcome to the Employee Management System (EMS)! This guide walks you through everything you need to know about using the application, whether you are an everyday Employee logging your hours, or an Administrator managing the workforce.

---

## 1. Getting Started

### Creating an Account
1. Open the EMS portal in your web browser.
2. At the bottom of the Login card, click **"Don't have an account? Sign Up"**.
3. Fill in your Full Name, Email, Password, Department, and Position.
4. Click **"Create Account"**. Note: Your account will be created with standard Employee access by default.

### Logging In
1. Navigate to the login screen.
2. Enter your registered email address and password.
3. Click **"Sign In"**. You will be securely logged into the dashboard.

---

## 2. Employee Features

As an Employee, your view is tailored to manage your personal day-to-day workflow.

### Dashboard & Attendance
- **Summary Cards**: Quickly see your status for the day, your remaining leave balance (starts at 20 days annually), and how many leaves have been approved.
- **Punch In / Punch Out**: Use the quick actions on the dashboard to log your time. 
  - Click **Punch In** when you start work.
  - Click **Punch Out** when your shift is over.
- **Attendance History**: Navigate to the **Attendance** tab on the left sidebar to view a complete log of your work history. If you forget to punch out for a day, the system will flag it with a red **"Missed Punch Out"** badge.

### Task Management
- Navigate to the **Tasks** tab to view tasks that have been assigned to you by administrators.
- You can filter and see the Priority (Low, Medium, High) and Due Date for each task.
- **Updating Status**: Use the dropdown menu in the "Action" column to update the status of your tasks (e.g., from *Pending* to *In Progress* or *Completed*).

### Leave Management
- Navigate to the **Leaves** tab to manage your time off.
- Click **"Apply Leave"** to open the leave application form. Provide a Start Date, End Date, and Reason.
- Your submitted leaves will show as *Pending* until an administrator reviews them. If approved, the days will be automatically deducted from your annual leave balance.

### Profile Management
- Navigate to the **Profile** tab to view your current details.
- Click **"Update Profile"** to change your Name, Department, Designation, or Contact Number. Simply use the checkboxes to unlock the fields you wish to change.

---

## 3. Administrator Features

Administrators have access to elevated controls, allowing them to oversee the entire workforce.

### The Admin Dashboard
- When you log in as an Admin, your dashboard cards will show company-wide statistics: **Total Employees**, **Pending Leaves**, and **Present Today**.

### Employee Management
- The specialized **Admin Panel** tab lists all registered users in the system alongside their respective details and remaining leave balances. 

### Managing Leaves
- Under the **Leaves** tab, administrators can view leave requests from *all* employees.
- Use the green Checkmark button to **Approve** a leave, or the red X button to **Reject** it. Approving a leave will automatically deduct the calculated days from the employee's balance.

### Assigning Tasks
- Under the **Tasks** tab, administrators have access to an exclusive **"Assign Task"** button.
- Clicking this opens a modal where you can select a specific employee from a dynamically updated dropdown list.
- You can set the Task Title, Description, Due Date, and Priority before assigning it directly to their personal workflow.
- You will see a master table of all assigned tasks, allowing you to track company-wide progress.
